RideWindow — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Summary

RideWindow is designed with privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data we access

Location

RideWindow requests your device location to fetch local weather forecasts. Your coordinates are sent directly to the weather service (Open-Meteo) to retrieve forecast data. We do not store, log, or share your location.

Calendar (optional)

If you choose to connect Google Calendar, RideWindow reads your calendar events to determine your availability for cycling. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to any server controlled by us.

Data we do NOT collect

  • No account or login required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No data shared with third parties
  • No server-side storage of any user data

Third-party services

  • Open-Meteo — receives your coordinates to provide weather data. See their privacy policy.
  • Google Calendar API (optional) — used only when you explicitly connect your calendar. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data storage

All app data (preferences, availability settings, cached weather) is stored locally on your device using the app's private storage. Uninstalling the app removes all data.

Children

RideWindow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
